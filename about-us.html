Mentorship & Entrepreneurship

Business mentoring refers to a relationship between an experienced business person (the mentor) and a business owner or employee (the mentee). Through mentoring you can develop your business skills for free, which can help you achieve success earlier than you may have on your own.Mentoring is based on equality, openness and trust. Above all, be very supportive.
